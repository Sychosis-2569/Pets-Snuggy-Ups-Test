About Us

This journey started during Covid, when life slowed down and I began noticing how many pets needed a comfortable space that was truly their own. With three of my own fur-babies (who are more like children to me!), I understood how much comfort, warmth, and love mean to them.

I began hand-making pet beds, each one stitched with care, attention to detail, and plenty of love. What makes these beds different is that they’re designed to fit perfectly into Petzone’s plastic beds, making them both practical and cozy. They’re durable, easy to clean, and made to last — while still giving your pets that soft, snuggly spot they deserve.

I am also able to make custom cushions. For example, for imported and woven beds. Sets can also be requested, (bed, cushion, pillows and blankets).

What started as a small idea has grown into something very close to my heart: creating cozy spaces where pets feel safe and happy, and giving fellow pet parents the peace of mind that their four-legged family members are truly cared for.
